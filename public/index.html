🛠️ Installation Instructions

Step 1: Add to your Shopify theme

Copy the following script and paste it before the closing </body> tag in your theme.liquid file:

<script src="https://shopify-whatsapp-app-m6cy.onrender.com/api/whatsapp-script"></script>

Step 2: Customize (Optional)

The widget will automatically use default settings. For advanced customization, you can modify the script after adding it to your theme.
